Help for Calculator:

Elementary funcitons:
* all trigonometric functions(sin, sinh,
arcsin, arcsinh, cos, cosh, arccos, arccosh,
tan, tanh, arctan, arctanh)
* exp(x) or e^x
* pow(a, b), a**b or a^b
* sqrt(x)
* root(x, n)
* ln(x)
* log(x, n)

Functions:
* d/dx(f(x))
* d^n/dx^n(f(x))  (exact derivative of f)
* d^n/dx^n(f(x))(x_0)   (Approximatee derivative of f in x_0)
* Int(a, b, f(x), x) (with selection of approximation method)

Arithmetic:
* C(a, b) or aCb
* fact(n) or n!
* KgV(a, b) or PPCM(a, b)
* ggT(a, b) or PGCD(a, b)
* isprime(n)
* eratosthenes(n)
* primfactors(n)
* partition(n)

Equations:
* a=b (check equality)
* f(x) = g(x) (solve equation for x)
